About

I help teams make complex software feel clear, usable, and reliable.

My focus is on understanding real-world workflows, finding friction in complex systems, and translating those insights into UX recommendations that teams can actually ship. I work end-to-end — from planning and running research to synthesizing results and aligning stakeholders on next steps.

Usability testing Workflow analysis Expert users Internal tools Enterprise & B2B Data-heavy UIs AI-assisted systems Remote & field

What you can expect

  • Clear research plans: goals, tasks, success criteria, and a realistic timeline
  • Solid execution: moderated sessions, clean notes, and careful handling of participant data
  • Useful synthesis: themes + severity/priority, backed by direct evidence
  • Practical outputs: findings that translate into product decisions and dev-ready next steps

Focus

Where I’m strongest Products where complexity is unavoidable: internal tools, enterprise software, configuration-heavy systems, and data-heavy workflows.
What I look for Points where users hesitate, misinterpret system feedback, or take “wrong turns” — and what needs to change in UI structure, language, and flow to prevent that.
How I define impact Fewer errors in critical steps, faster completion of key tasks, lower cognitive load, and clearer decisions for product teams.
How I work with teams Early alignment on the decision we need to make, quick feedback loops during the study, and a readout that ends with priorities and next actions.

Methods

  • Moderated usability tests (remote + on-site)
  • A/B comparisons (within-subjects, counterbalancing)
  • Think-aloud, observation, task analysis
  • Expert interviews (evaluative and workflow-focused)
  • Cognitive walkthroughs and heuristic reviews
  • Surveys + standardized questionnaires (NASA-TLX, ISO 9241-110 / ISONORM)
  • Synthesis: affinity mapping, thematic analysis, severity ratings

Research operations

  • Study plans: objectives, hypotheses, tasks, success criteria
  • Screeners, recruitment workflows, participant tracking
  • Consent and GDPR-minded data handling
  • Session logistics (field setups, hardware, recording)
  • Moderator scripts, checklists, structured note-taking
  • Readouts: findings + evidence + prioritization
