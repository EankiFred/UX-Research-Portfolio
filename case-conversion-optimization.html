Case study • Conversion optimization

UX research for conversion optimization projects

I supported multiple conversion optimization projects by analyzing usability test data and translating qualitative insights into clear, actionable reports for stakeholders. The goal was to identify friction points in the user journey and provide concrete recommendations to improve conversion, clarity, and trust.

At a glance

Scope Multiple usability studies across conversion-focused product flows
Methods Unmoderated usability tests, think-aloud, behavioral analysis

Summary

What I did Analysis of usability test recordings and transcripts, identification of behavioral patterns, synthesis of insights, and creation of structured reports for stakeholders.
Goal Reduce friction in key user journeys, increase clarity and trust, and improve conversion-related decision making.

Context

Work was conducted in a conversion optimization context across multiple digital products. Due to confidentiality agreements, specific client details cannot be disclosed.

Approach

Research workflow

  • Review of usability test sessions (video + transcripts)
  • Identification of friction points across user journeys
  • Clustering of issues via thematic analysis
  • Prioritization based on severity and business impact

Output

  • Clear, structured reports for stakeholders
  • Evidence-backed insights (quotes + behavior)
  • Actionable recommendations for design and product
  • Focus on clarity, trust, and decision support

Key insights

Clarity issues Users often struggled to understand product value, positioning, or key benefits after first exposure.
Trust barriers Missing or hard-to-find information reduced perceived credibility and willingness to continue.
Information hierarchy Important content was often placed too late in the journey, reducing discoverability.
Overly sales-driven flows Strong push towards conversion without sufficient understanding reduced engagement.

Impact

Typical outcomes Research findings informed optimization experiments that contributed to measurable improvements in conversion rates and user engagement.
My contribution Turning complex qualitative data into clear, prioritized insights that enabled faster and more confident product decisions.

Note: Specific performance metrics and client details are omitted due to confidentiality agreements.
