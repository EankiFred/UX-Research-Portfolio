NetLogCom — Enhancing usability in environmental monitoring

In collaboration with SEBA Hydrometrie GmbH & Co. KG and de-build.net POWER GmbH, I analyzed and redesigned the NetLogCom software interface to make it easier to use—especially for less experienced users. I built a high-fidelity prototype and validated it through on-site usability testing with 35 domain experts.

Methods Cognitive walkthroughs • high-fidelity prototyping • A/B usability testing (within-subjects) • think-aloud • NASA-TLX • ISONORM 9241/110-S • affinity mapping • severity ratings
Outcome Prototype performed significantly better than the original in effectiveness, efficiency, and satisfaction.
